Introduction

This tool captures and analyses a bunch of data about the repositories within a GitHub organisation.

The goal is to identify potential security improvements in terms of:

  • configuring active monitoring and patching.
  • identifying known vulnerabilities
  • identifying un-archived and un-maintained repositories
