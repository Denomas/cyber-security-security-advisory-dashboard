In order to add your repository to dependabot there are a number of steps to follow with some differences in configuration based on the repository. The steps to take are as follows:

  1. Navigate to dependabot.com and click Login
  2. Allow GitHub access to your account when prompted on logging into dependabot.com
  3. Click on the Add repo button in the top left corner
  4. If your repository is not listed, click on the Grant access to additional repos button
  5. Once you find your repository in the list, check the checkbox and click Add selected, found in the top right corner
  6. If your dependency files are not found in the root of the repository, you will need to add a .dependabot/config.yml to the root
.dependabot/config.yml example
version: 1

update_configs:
- package_manager: "ruby:bundler"
directory: "/common"
update_schedule: "live"
default_reviewers:
- greysteil

- package_manager: "php:composer"
directory: "/composer/helpers"
update_schedule: "live"

- package_manager: "elixir:hex"
directory: "/hex/helpers"
update_schedule: "live"

- package_manager: "javascript"
directory: "/npm_and_yarn/helpers"
update_schedule: "live"

- package_manager: "python"
directory: "/python/helpers"
update_schedule: "live"

- package_manager: "go:modules"
directory: "/go_modules/helpers"
update_schedule: "daily"
